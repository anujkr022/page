Tourist Places in Rishikesh

Rishikesh, also known as the world's Yoga Capital, is extremely popular among tourists around the globe. If you're someone who's into yoga, visiting historic
temples, and trying adventurous activities, there are several places to visit in Rishikesh, all of which are included in Thrillophilia Rishikesh Tour Packages. Located by the Ganga River, the town holds a special place in the hearts of tourists for its peaceful and spiritual ambience.
For those who're up for a temple tour, Neelkanth Mahadev Temple and Raghunath Temple are some of the most religious places to visit in Rishikesh. Travellers who're into architecture can admire the 3 bridges in the town that are named after Hindu Gods and Goddesses - Ram Jhula, Laxman Jhula, and Sita Jhula.
Do songs like 'Hey Jude' and 'Let It Be' by the Beatles get you humming along? If yes, then remember to visit the Beatles Ashram, which is one of the most unique places to visit in Rishikesh. Speaking of unique places to visit, how would you feel about climbing 13 floors? If that sounds like an adventure to you, Tera Manzil Temple is the perfect place for you to explore in Rishikesh. The temple, according to sources, was built in the 12th century and houses many idols of Hindu gods and goddesses.
If you're into yoga and meditation,you can head to Swarg Ashram, where travellers from different corners of the world gather for some quiet time. And yes, we can hear you, adventure seekers! You can head out on a trek to Kunjapuri Temple, which is a tourist favourite as it offers fantastic views of the town from the top. Moreover, you can also try white-water rafting in Rishikesh along the challenging rapids or enjoy the Neer Garh Waterfall Trek for a combination of thrill and outstanding views.
For travellers seeking spiritual tourist places in Rishikesh, Triveni Ghat offers an experience like no other. Hundreds of locals and travellers gather at the shores of the holy river to be a part of the Ganga Aarti - it's an experience which is difficult to describe in words.
Tourist places in Rishikesh have something for everyone, from those seeking peace to those looking for fun and adventure.
